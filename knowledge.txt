DIGITALSIGHT CLIENT DASHBOARD KNOWLEDGE BASE
==========================================

Welcome to the DigitalSight Distribution Platform. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the dashboard functions, release creation process, and system statuses.

1. DASHBOARD OVERVIEW
---------------------
The Dashboard is your central command center. It provides:
- Release Statistics: Real-time count of your releases by status (Draft, Published, Pending, etc.).
- Entity Statistics: Number of Artists and Labels under your account.
- Corporate Board Notices: Important system updates, policy changes, and announcements.
- Quick Actions: Fast access to "My Catalog" and "Manage Artists".

2. CORE PAGES & FUNCTIONS
-------------------------
- Dashboard: Overview of your account activity and statistics.
- My Catalog (Releases): View and manage all your music submissions.
- Artists: Create and manage artist profiles. Each release must be linked to an artist.
- Labels / Sub-Labels: Manage your publishing entities and hierarchy.
- Correction Queue: Specifically for releases marked as "Needs Info" that require your attention.
- Notices: A dedicated space for all official communications from DigitalSight.
- Support & FAQ: Access help documentation or contact the support team.
- Settings: Manage your profile, password, and account preferences.

3. CREATING A RELEASE (STEP-BY-STEP)
------------------------------------
Creating a release involves a 7-step process to ensure all metadata is accurate for global distribution.

Step 1: General Information
- Album Title: The official name of the release.
- Album Type: Choose between Album or Film (Single).
- Content Type: Single, Album, Compilation, or Remix.
- Artists: Select Primary and Featured artists from your managed list.

Step 2: Commercial Metadata
- UPC/EAN: Universal Product Code. Leave blank to auto-generate.
- Catalogue Number: Your internal tracking number (e.g., DS-001).
- Release Date: Must be at least 3 DAYS from the current date.
- P-Line & C-Line: Phonographic and Composition copyright lines (e.g., 2024 DigitalSight).
- Publisher: The entity owning the publishing rights.

Step 3: Genre & Mood
- Primary & Sub-Genre: Accurate categorization helps in store placement.
- Core Mood: Helps in playlisting (e.g., Happy, Energetic).
- Language: The primary language of the lyrics.
- Parental Advisory: Mark as "Explicit" if the content contains strong language.

Step 4: Film Sync (Optional)
- Metadata for cinematic releases, including Director, Producer, Studio, and Star Cast.

Step 5: Tracklist
- Track Title & Version: (e.g., "Song Name (Radio Edit)").
- ISRC: International Standard Recording Code. Leave blank to auto-generate.
- Composer & Lyricist: Full legal names are mandatory.
- Audio Upload: High-fidelity WAV masters only.
- CRBT: Details for Caller Ring Back Tones (Title and Duration).

Step 6: Artwork
- Requirements: 3000 x 3000 pixels, Square, JPEG or PNG format.
- YouTube Content ID: Option to enable digital fingerprinting protection.

Step 7: Review & Submit
- Final Review: Check all data for accuracy.
- Submission Note: Add any technical context for the auditors.
- Execute Ingest: Submit the release for review.

4. RELEASE STATUSES
-------------------
- Draft: Saved locally. Not yet submitted for review.
- Pending: Submitted and waiting for the DigitalSight team to audit.
- Needs Info: Correction requested. Check the "Journal Memo" or "Admin Notes" for details.
- Approved: Passed audit and ready for ingestion.
- Processed: Successfully ingested into the distribution system.
- Published: The release is LIVE on digital stores (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.).
- Rejected: Denied for distribution (usually due to copyright or quality issues).
- Takedown: The release has been removed from stores.
- Cancelled: Submission cancelled by the user or admin.

5. WAITING PERIODS & SUBMISSION
-------------------------------
- Minimum Lead Time: Releases must be submitted at least 3 days before the release date. For best results and playlist consideration, 2-3 weeks is recommended.
- Review Time: The DigitalSight team typically reviews submissions within 24-72 hours.
- Store Processing: Once "Processed", stores may take 24-48 hours to reflect the release.

6. REQUIRED DATA CHECKLIST
--------------------------
Before submitting, ensure you have:
[ ] High-quality WAV audio files.
[ ] 3000x3000px Cover Art.
[ ] Legal names of all Composers and Lyricists.
[ ] Artist IDs (Spotify/Apple) if available.
[ ] Correct P-Line and C-Line information.
